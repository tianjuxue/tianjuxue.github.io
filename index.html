I'm a postdoc at Northwestern University working with Prof. Jian Cao in the Advanced Manufacturing Processes Laboratory. I obtained my Ph.D. from Princeton University where I was co-advised by Prof. Sigrid Adriaenssens in the Form Finding Lab and Prof. Ryan Adams in the Laboratory for Intelligent Probabilistic Systems. My research interests are at the intersection of machine learning, computational mechanics, and additive manufacturing.
